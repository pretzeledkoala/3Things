# $\mathbb{A}^1$-homotopy theory 

Morel-Voevodsky constructed 
$$ 
\text{SH}(k)=\text{ stable }\mathbb{A}^1\text{-homotopy theory}
$$
which is powerful because it takes in both smooth $k$-schemes and topological spaces.
